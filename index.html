Hi there!

I'm Sneha, and I am a Software Development Engineer at Amazon in Seattle, Washington.

I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Master's in Computer Science in 2017 and from PES University with a Bachelor's degree in Information Science in 2015, at the age of 20. I have previously worked for Audible, Pluribus Networks, VMware, and Avaya as a Software Engineer Intern.

I am passionate about AI and working with data. My research focus at Penn was in the fields of Machine Learning, NLP, and Deep learning. Master's thesis at Penn was an application of Deep learning for a Natural Language Understanding problem. I am also very passionate about supporting women in technology and I'm currently working towards helping students in STEM fields to find mentorship and support.
